    Meaning

    Animals are very important in human life. They are used as food, traction power, industrial raw material and others. Some animals are harmful to one or another branch of the public economy. Their role in preserving the environment as it is today is worth mentioning. A large part of the population does not understand this importance, and this is the main reason for the problems faced by the animal world. Many species are extirpated or threatened with extinction due to unsparing destruction and improper interference in natural processes. Currently, effective measures are being taken to regulate, protect, and reproduce the species of animals (laws have been issued, nature reserves have been established, prohibitions have been introduced, the "Red Book" has been introduced, etc.). However, judging by the results, it turns out that the progression of animal world degradation approaches geometric progression. There is an opinion that the process of degeneration of nature, which is so obvious, is one of the stages of evolutionary development, and therefore the disappearance of species or entire ecosystems is not a sign that the entire living nature is threatened. But until such assumptions take the form of a theory (although it may never happen), humanity is taking a big risk.
